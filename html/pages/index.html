Student Success Activities

Student Success activities are things that Students do on Monday during Collaboration. These include doing activities and talking about bullying awareness, the growth mindset, and things like that.

Check it Out

Flex Fridays

Flex Fridays are activities organized by your SLT that promote health and wellbeing. These activities can range from mini-olympics to intramurals, and even cup-stacking challenges!

Check it Out

Wellness Wednesdays

Wellness Wednesdays are activities that are organized by your local CP. These can include different clubs, or intramurals, or academic time. These days are very much enjoyed by students.
